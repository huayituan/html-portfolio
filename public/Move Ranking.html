Top Chinese Comedy Movies

Top 3 movies in my opinion

Nine-Pin Sesame Official --- Stephen Chow

One of Stephen Chow's masterpieces with strong comedic effects.

The Deer Tripods --- Stephen Chow

Jin Yong's famous novel, Stephen Chow's rendition of Wei-Shou-Bou is cheap and cute but affectionate, the movie is full of laughs.

Lost in Translation - Xu Zheng and Wang Baoqiang

A low-budget comedy movie with a cost of $6 million in exchange for $37 million at the box office, the audience is more able to experience the real Chinese Spring Festival scene from it!
